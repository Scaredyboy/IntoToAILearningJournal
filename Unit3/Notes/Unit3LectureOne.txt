What is multimedia
	Any format of media - Video game, picture, video, speech
	
Key terms
	text-to-speech:
		- Converting written text-to-speech(tts)
	voice cloning:
		- Use a tool to convert text to sound like a sepcific voice
	text-to-video
		- Using a prompt to create a video
	image-to-video
		- Using an image that turns into a moving video
		
for engineering prompts
	for audio give it an emotion for the video, explain what type of audio
	for video use cinamatic terms, camera angle, lighting, style: documentary, artistic
